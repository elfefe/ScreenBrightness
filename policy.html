ScreenBrightness Security and Policy

Privacy Policy

At ScreenBrightness, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your data and ensuring that your personal information is handled with care and in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Data Collection

We may collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version).
  • App usage data (e.g., app settings and preferences).

Note: We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless explicitly provided by you (e.g., through feedback or support forms).

Data Usage

The data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Improving app performance and user experience.
  • Fixing bugs and troubleshooting technical issues.
  • Developing new features and functionalities.

Security Measures

We employ advanced security practices to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive information during data transmission.
  • Regular updates to address security vulnerabilities.
  • Limited access to data for authorized personnel only.

User Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we collect about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data where applicable.
  • Opt-Out: Disable data collection through the app settings.

Third-Party Services

ScreenBrightness may use third-party services to enhance functionality (e.g., analytics, ads). These services are governed by their respective privacy policies. We ensure that our partners adhere to strict privacy and security standards.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy as needed to reflect changes in legal requirements or app features. Users will be notified of significant updates through app notifications or email.
